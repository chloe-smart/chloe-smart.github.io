About Me

Hello! I'm Chloe, I am a driven, creative, enthusiastic full-stack developer. In my spare time I love drawing, live music & cooking. Whilst working as a designer, I always enjoyed learning new technologies & CAD to aid in my design skills. I first became interested in coding whilst building a website for my business - I loved thinking about how to make the best possible experience for the user. I decided to do the Full-Stack Track course at iO Academy as I was eager to turn my passion for coding into a career in software development.

Portfolio

Whilst studying software development at iO Academy I worked on a range of projects, both individual & in a scrum team. Below is a small section of my favourite projects, have a look at my portfolio for the rest!

React - Food Delivery App

We built a food delivery app using React & Bootstrap, fetching the restaurants & their menus from an existing RESTful API. I worked on fetching the menu for a specific restaurant when selected & changing the components on the page to display the menu as Bootstrap cards. I also worked as the Scrum Master for this project, this taught me leadership\ facilitation skills & how to handle challenging situations. As well as developing the tools to enable me to communicate & listen to each member of the team.
